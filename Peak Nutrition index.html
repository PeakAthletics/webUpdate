What can we do at Peak Nutrition?

Through metabolic testing, we can evaluate the level of nutrient absorption assimilation and determine it at a cellular level. We also review the genetic makeup of each athlete to look at polymorphisms that might hinder performance. With this information, we can guide the athlete to enhance the assimilation of nutrients and favorable expression of genes.
Deficiencies can lead to fatigue, lack of mental performance, and lack of recovery and healing from training. All athletes are potentially susceptible to deficiencies due to gastric distress, malabsorption, and intestinal permeability.
